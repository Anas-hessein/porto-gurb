About

I'm a developer passionate about building creative projects that help me expand what is currently just a drop in the ocean of this vast and ever-evolving field. I find great joy in learning at the intersection of design and development, where every project presents a new opportunity to grow and refine my skills.

Currently, I'm a junior member at Hackclub, specializing in front-end web development. I aspire to become an active contributor to Hackclub Egypt and help expand the reach of its activities across a wider community — through initiatives like Scrapyard, the biggest hackathon in Egypt, and other national events that have attracted a huge number of participants.

My ultimate goal is to contribute to meaningful tech communities, build impactful projects, and keep growing as a developer who inspires and supports others on their learning journey.

In my spare time, I usually pray, hang out with my friends, or train at the gymgym.
